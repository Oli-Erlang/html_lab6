Release Management Lead release activities in coordination with products, project teams, DevOps, content managers and other departments. Establish artifacts, requirements, limitations, schedule, plan and risks of releases. Define technical and business dependencies of releases. Run advisory boards with release stakeholders and notify all involved parties on release plans. Assemble release teams and supervise their preparation for the release.
